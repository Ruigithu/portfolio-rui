How MySQL search for data?


March 26, 2025 by Rui Peng

This blog post shows what kind of strategy and data structure MySQL has used to search data as fast as posible.

Index

Before we talk about the technical terms, let's think about a scene that you have a textbok without catalog and you are asked by your teacher to turn to page of 'Unit 3'. There are two ways tohelp you find it, one is page by page, two is turning page randomly. This process might be really slowly when your textbook is very thick and you are not that lucky.

To some extent, our database is the book, every page can be viewed as a data record, its amount may reach tens of millions. Finding the right record (Full Table Scan) may take a really long time which will rail user up who is requesting the data. So to speed the process and satisfy the users, we have to construct 'catalog' for users, which is the Index.
